NSDA Pass Moments

Certified Web Developer by Prime Minister's Office

I am very happy to share that I have been officially recognized as a Certified Frontend Web Developer & Designer (Level-3) by the National Skills Development Authority (NSDA) of the Government of the People's Republic of Bangladesh.

Behind this achievement is a story of hard work. The course that is usually given a long time of 3 months (90 days) to complete, I have completed the entire syllabus in just 19 days through hard work, day and night, without sleeping!

Capitalizing on this government recognition and confidence, I am going to start a new and big chapter of my life. Every line of code, nightly practice, and the mentality of not giving up — the result of everything is today's achievement.

BIUPC 2024

Hosted By BUBT IT Club

The BIUPC 2024 (BUBT Inter-University Programming Contest) was a massive milestone event for competitive programmers, hosted by the BUBT IT Club at the Bangladesh University of Business and Technology (BUBT) campus, where I proudly competed in the Junior division.

Breaking records with over 1,600 participants across Junior and Senior categories, it promoted algorithmic problem-solving and coding excellence. My team "fitar" performed remarkably well by solving 4 problems and securing the 48th place in the competition.
